2.Body

How do I start the main part/body/details?

2.0 State the main point

Clearly inform your audience what the main point is for each part of the presentation
清楚的告訴聽眾簡報每個部份的要點是甚麼
Say why it is important or interesting
說明為甚麼他很重要或有趣
Provide evidence: Use numbers for prices, percentages, statistics, sizes, distance, and time.
提供證據: 使用數字表示價格、百分比、統計數據、尺寸、大小、距離和時間
Use examples for quality, comfort, beauty, or things that are difficult to measure.
使用範例表示質量、舒適度、美觀或難以測量的事物
Avoid long explanations.
避免太長的解釋
Only show the main parts of a diagram or process. Avoid too much detail. It confuses the
audience.
僅顯示圖表或流程的要點。避免太多細節。會使聽眾感到困惑。
Try to avoid using complex formulas, equations, and calculations. They can confuse or
distract the audience.
嘗試避免使用複雜的公式、方程式和計算。他們會使聽眾感到困惑或分心。
